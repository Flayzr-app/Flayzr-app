Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Flayzr

Q: What is this app for?

This app lets you generate hilarious AI-powered roasts, clever comebacks, and vibe checks from your photos. It's all in good fun—perfect for entertaining your friends or crafting witty replies.

Q: How can it help me deal with online bullying or awkward conversations?

Many people struggle with responding to negativity or rude comments online. Our comeback generator gives you smart, sharp, and mature responses to shut down negativity without being toxic—helping you stand your ground with confidence and humor.

Q: Is my data and privacy safe?

Absolutely. We use strong encryption and never store your images after processing. Your email and name are kept secure and are only used to sync your account across devices.

Q: How do credits work?

You get 10 free credits every day. Each roast, comeback, or vibe check uses 1 credit. Need more? Watch a short ad to earn extra credits anytime.

Q: Can I use the app on multiple devices?

Yes! Just log in with the same email, and all your roasts, history, and vibe results will sync automatically.

Q: What happens if I delete my account?

All your data—including images and history—will be permanently deleted from our servers.

Q: Are the roasts and comebacks appropriate for all ages?

The AI is trained to keep things clever and lighthearted. Still, users are responsible for how and where they use the generated content.

Q: Do you plan to add more features?

Yes! We're always working on new roast styles, vibe categories, and social features based on user feedback.

Q: I'm having technical issues. What should I do?

Reach out to our support team via the app settings. We're happy to help!

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 09/10/2025

Data Collection & Purpose

When you sign up for our app, we collect your email address and display name to create your account and sync your activity across devices. This allows you to access your roast history, saved content, uploaded images, and preferences no matter where you log in. We only collect the data we need to operate the app, personalize your experience, and continue improving our services. We do not collect any sensitive personal information without your permission.

Types of Data Collected

Personal Information:

  • Email addresses (for account creation)
  • User IDs (for app functionality)

Usage Data:

  • Product interaction data
  • Advertising data (via Google AdMob)
  • Performance and crash data

Device Information:

  • Device identifiers (for advertising)
  • Photos (temporarily for AI processing)

Image Storage & Security

Your uploaded images are stored in our encrypted backend database and are accessible only by you through your logged-in account. Our development team has no access to view or use your personal photos. We use modern encryption and secure data storage practices to protect your media and ensure that your privacy is never compromised. Your content is yours—private, protected, and never shared without your consent.

How We Use Your Data

App Functionality:

  • User authentication and account management
  • AI processing of uploaded photos
  • Cross-device synchronization
  • Crash reporting and performance monitoring

Third-Party Advertising:

  • Displaying ads through Google AdMob
  • Advertising performance measurement

Analytics:

  • Understanding feature usage patterns
  • Improving user experience
  • Measuring audience characteristics

Cross-Device Synchronization

By signing in with the same email on different devices, your content—including roasts, vibe checks, and uploaded images—is automatically synced in real time. This means you can switch phones or reinstall the app without losing anything. All data transfers are encrypted, and your experience remains seamless across devices, so you can pick up exactly where you left off.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob:

  • Used for displaying advertisements
  • Collects device identifiers and advertising data
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Supabase:

  • Backend database and authentication
  • Stores user accounts and app data
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Data Control & Deletion

You're in full control of your data. From within the app settings, you can delete individual images or your entire account and all associated content. When you delete anything, it's permanently removed from our servers—no backups, no recovery. We do not retain deleted content, ensuring your privacy and control are always respected.

Your Privacy Choices

Opt-out Options:

  • Disable personalized ads in device settings
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Request data deletion via email

Access Rights:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Export your data upon request

Third-Party Data & Advertising

We work with trusted ad partners who may collect anonymous usage data (like in-app behavior) to show you relevant ads. This data does not include your personal info and cannot identify you. You can opt out of personalized ads anytime through your device settings. We never sell your personal information to anyone. Any data we collect is only used to improve your experience within the app.

International Users

GDPR Compliance:

European Union users have additional rights under GDPR including data portability and the right to be forgotten.

CCPA/CPRA Compliance:

California residents can opt-out of data sharing and request disclosure of data collection practices.

CalOPPA:

We comply with California Online Privacy Protection Act requirements for transparency.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.
